EE140 — Analog IC Design Lab: Folded-Cascode Amplifier

UC Berkeley

Designed a folded-cascode op-amp meeting closed-loop gain, phase margin, PSRR/CMRR and settling-time targets under process/voltage/temperature variations; verified in Spectre with Monte-Carlo and AC/transient sweeps.

  • Focus: gain-bandwidth trade-off, compensation, biasing for robustness.
  • Checks: AC gain/PM, step response (settling), PSRR/CMRR, noise estimate.

EE143 — Microfabrication Lab: Process Flow & Test Chips

UC Berkeley

Hands-on microfabrication: lithography, etching, oxidation, deposition; fabricated devices (transistors/resistors/diodes/capacitors) and measured electrical characteristics to close the loop with device-level models.
