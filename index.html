Analog / Mixed-Signal IC

Hi, I’m Shentao. I design data converters and high-speed interfaces.

Focus areas: ADC/ΔΣ, SerDes & high-speed I/O, PLL/clocking, cryo-CMOS, and AI-for-Analog. Recent work includes a Bandgap Reference (BGR) and a Transimpedance Amplifier (TIA) interfacing a MEMS-based oscillator — both slated for silicon tape-out (SKY130).

Currently B.Eng. @ ShanghaiTech; exchange @ UC Berkeley. I enjoy combining device/circuit intuition with ML-assisted flows to make analog design faster and more robust.
